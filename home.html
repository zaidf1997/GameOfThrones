What is GOT?

A long time ago , several royal families of a desire the 'Iron Throne' the highest of the thrones to gain control of Westeros. Brave warriors fight for the king and their honour, while their kings are traitors to their people and their faith.
With the fall in power of the Lannisters followed by the death of all the Lannistors sons, the chaos and the fight for the throne is rising.
The dragon queen , Daenerys Targaryen , is on a quest to attack the city and take what is right fully hers. With the help of her three Dragons, she has risen to power across the Narrow Sea.
The King in the North, Jon Snow is aware of the upcoming threat , The White Walkers ,who have risen after centuries to kill the humans
. Will the walkers take down the Wall and enter Westeros to create chaos and havoc ? Will the warriors fight the walkers for their lives and send them back to the darkness for the newx few centuries ?
Keep watching GOT and stay tuned on this website for more theories.
